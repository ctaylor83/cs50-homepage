Waffles

Ingredients

Click each ingredient to strike off as required
  • 1 level cup of plain flour
  • 2 tablespoons of granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted, melted butter

Method

Click each step to strike off as done
  • 1. Put the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt into a bowl and Mix
  • 2. Whisk the milk and eggs together thebpour over the flour mixture
  • 3. Begin to add the butter and whisk into the mixture
  • 4. Pour mixture into the waffle iron and cook
