Fruit and Veg Chutney

Ingredients

Click each ingredient to strike off as required
  • 1kg of Fruit & Veg of your choosing
  • 500g White onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2in Piece of ginger OR 2tbsp Ginger puree
  • 100g Sultanas
  • Dried chilli flakes to taste
  • 500ml Distilled malt vinegar
  • 250g Brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp of salt

Method

Click each step to strike off as done
  • 1. Roughly chop the fruit & veg
  • 2. Finely chop the onion, garlic and ginger
  • 3. Place all ingredients im a saucepan on a medium heat until the sugar is dissolved
  • 4. Once sugar is dissolved bring to the boil then reduce heat to a simmer
  • 5. Simmer for minimum of 1hr stirrimg every so often
  • 6. The chutney will be ready when you can scrape a wooden spoon across the bottom of the pan and it will not flow into the gap
  • 7. Spoon chutney into clean glass jars and seal while hot
  • 8. Store in a cool dark cupboard for at least a month to let the flavours fuse
